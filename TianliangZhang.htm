Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate of Signal and Information Processing in the School of Electronic, Electrical and Communication Engineering, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences , advised by Prof. Qixiang Ye. I got my Master Degree in 2017 at PRISDL in the School of Engineering Science, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Qixiang Ye, and B.Eng. degree from Wuhan University of Technology (WUT) advised by Prof. Qingsong Ai in 2013.

My research interests include computer vision and machine learning, specifically for self-learning, pedestrian detection, and visual object detection.
